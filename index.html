How PaladPay Works

Our revolutionary palm-vein biometric payment system makes transactions simpler than ever.

1

One-Time Enrollment

Register your palm print with valid ID (preferably PhilSys ID) and link it to your payment method(s) (GCash, Maya, or other).

2

Simple Authentication

To pay, users simply hover their palm over the scanner. No phone, no cards, no problem!

3

Instant Verification

Terminal identifies user, verifies identity, and processes transaction in under 500ms.

4

Fund Settlement

Transaction is processed through connected financial networks (requires merchant connectivity).

About PaladPay

PaladPay is a pioneering Filipino fintech startup founded in 2025 by Rafael Harnisch (CEO) and Raven Aguirre (CTO). We're on a mission to revolutionize how Filipinos make payments by introducing cutting-edge palm vein recognition technology.

Our vision is to make secure, seamless, and accessible payments possible for every Filipino, even those without smartphone ownership. We believe financial inclusion should not be limited by device ownership or connectivity.

With over 30 million unbanked Filipinos and persistent connectivity challenges across the country, PaladPay addresses key barriers to financial inclusion through our innovative biometric payment solution.
